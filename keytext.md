## Description

SnapSort AI is the smartest way to manage your screenshots. Powered by on-device AI, it automatically detects, classifies, and organizes every screenshot on your iPhone — so you never lose an important capture again.

🧠 AI-POWERED CLASSIFICATION
SnapSort AI uses Apple's Vision framework to automatically sort your screenshots into 13 smart categories:
• OTP Codes — One-time passwords and verification codes
• QR Codes — Scannable QR codes and barcodes
• Receipts — Purchase receipts and transaction records
• Delivery — Shipping and delivery tracking
• Chat — Chat conversations and messages
• Social Media — Social media posts and stories
• Maps — Location screenshots and directions
• Memes — Fun memes and reaction images
• Documents — Important documents and forms
• Payment — Payment confirmations and transfers
• Travel — Boarding passes, tickets, and itineraries
• Food — Restaurant menus and food orders
• Other — Everything else

🔍 OCR TEXT SEARCH
Search the actual text inside your screenshots. Find that verification code, address, or phone number in seconds — even if you forgot which screenshot it's in.

🧹 SMART CLEANUP
Temporary screenshots like OTP codes and delivery tracking get automatic cleanup reminders. Never let expired codes clutter your library again.

🔒 FACE ID PROTECTION
Lock sensitive categories like OTP codes and receipts behind Face ID. Your private screenshots stay private.

📱 100% ON-DEVICE PROCESSING
All AI classification and OCR run locally on your iPhone using Apple's Vision framework. Zero data collection. Zero cloud uploads. Your screenshots never leave your device.

📊 STORAGE INSIGHTS
See exactly how much space your screenshots take and reclaim storage with smart batch cleanup.

KEY FEATURES:
• Automatic screenshot detection from Photos
• 13 AI-powered smart categories
• OCR text recognition and search
• Smart cleanup reminders for temporary screenshots
• Face ID protection for sensitive categories
• Batch delete and organize
• On-device AI — no internet required
• Zero data collection — privacy first
