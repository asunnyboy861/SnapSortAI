Frequently Asked Questions

How does SnapSort AI classify my screenshots?

SnapSort AI uses Apple's on-device Vision framework and machine learning to automatically categorize your screenshots into 13 smart categories like OTP codes, QR codes, receipts, and more. All processing happens on your device — no data is ever uploaded.

Why can't SnapSort AI access my photos?

You need to grant photo library access when first opening the app. If you denied access, go to iOS Settings → SnapSort AI → Photos, and select "All Photos" or "Selected Photos".

How do cleanup reminders work?

Screenshots in temporary categories (OTP, Delivery Tracking, etc.) will trigger cleanup reminders after a configurable number of days (default: 3 days). You can adjust this in Settings.

Is my data safe?

Absolutely. All AI processing, OCR, and classification happen entirely on your device. We don't collect, transmit, or store any of your data on external servers. See our Privacy Policy for details.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. Go to Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions → SnapSort AI to cancel or change your subscription.

Can I recover deleted screenshots?

Deleted screenshots are moved to the "Recently Deleted" album in your Photos app, where they remain for 30 days before permanent deletion. SnapSort AI cannot recover permanently deleted screenshots.

Tips & Tricks

  • Use the Search tab to find screenshots by their text content using OCR
  • Enable Face ID in Settings to protect sensitive categories like OTP and Receipts
  • Tap "Select All" in Cleanup to quickly delete multiple temporary screenshots
  • Long-press a screenshot to change its category manually
