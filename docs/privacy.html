Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 4, 2026

TL;DR: SnapSort AI processes everything on your device. We don't collect, transmit, or store your personal data on any server.

1. Data Collection

SnapSort AI does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All screenshot analysis, OCR text recognition, and AI classification happen entirely on your device using Apple's built-in frameworks (Vision, Core ML).

2. Photo Library Access

SnapSort AI requests access to your photo library solely to:

  • Detect and display screenshots from your Photos app
  • Perform on-device OCR and classification
  • Allow you to organize and delete screenshots

Your photos are never uploaded, shared, or accessed by anyone other than you.

3. On-Device Processing

All AI features run locally on your device:

  • OCR text recognition (Vision framework)
  • Screenshot classification (on-device ML)
  • Duplicate detection
  • Category management

No data leaves your device at any time.

4. Face ID / Biometrics

If you enable Face ID protection, biometric authentication is handled entirely by iOS. We do not access, store, or process any biometric data.

5. Notifications

Cleanup reminder notifications are scheduled locally on your device. No notification data is sent to any server.

6. In-App Purchases

Subscription payments are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information. Apple's privacy policy applies to all transactions.

7. Analytics

SnapSort AI does not use any analytics services, tracking SDKs, or third-party advertising frameworks. We have zero visibility into how you use the app.

8. Children's Privacy

Our app does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
